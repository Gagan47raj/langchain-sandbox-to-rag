COMPANY OVERVIEW
TechMantra Solutions Pvt. Ltd. is a leading IT services and consulting company headquartered in Bangalore, India.
Founded in 2010 by Rajesh Kumar and Priya Sharma, the company has grown to 5,000+ employees across 6 global offices.
Annual revenue for FY 2023-24: ₹1,200 crores ($145 million USD).
