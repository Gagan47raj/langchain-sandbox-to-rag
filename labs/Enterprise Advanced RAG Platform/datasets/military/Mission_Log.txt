--- 0430 HOURS ---
Insertion via HALO jump successful. LZ cold. Full squad accounted 
for: Myself, "Mother" (Jenkins), "Prophet" (Okonkwo), "Grinch" 
(Mackie). Moving to OP Alpha. Comms check green across board.

--- 0515 HOURS ---
Contact. Small arms fire from the hospital complex. Prophet 
confirmed 4-5 hostiles, Al-Qatala insignia on armbands. Returned 
fire. Mother dropped two with the AX-50. Grinch and I pushed the 
stairwell. Frag out. Two more down. Room clear.

Prophet is hit. Took a 7.62 through the plate carrier shoulder 
gap. Not fatal but he's bleeding bad. Applied tourniquet. He's 
still combat effective but slowing us down. Called it in—Price 
says no medevac until HVT is secured. Copy that. Moving on.

--- 0620 HOURS ---
Reached the bunker entrance. Way heavier resistance than intel 
suggested. Counted at least 12 hostiles plus technical vehicles 
with mounted DShK. This isn't a holding force—this is a 
garrison. Someone knew we were coming.

Called in the airstrike. Danger close. The V-TOL made three 
passes. Absolute carnage. Grinch said it best: "War crime on 
toast." Bunker entrance is clear but now we've announced 
ourselves to every tango in a 5-click radius.

--- 0645 HOURS ---
HVT confirmed. "The Butcher" is in custody. Doesn't look so 
tough now with a broken jaw and zip-ties cutting into his 
wrists. Prophet wants to "accidentally" drop him down the 
stairs. Tempting, but Price wants him alive for interrogation.

--- 0710 HOURS ---
Exfil is hot. Three technicals inbound from the east. Mother is 
covering from the rooftop. Grinch rigged the approach with 
claymores. I've got the HVT. Helo ETA 4 minutes. It's gonna be 
tight.

Those 4 minutes felt like an hour. Claymores took out the first 
technical. Mother's .50 cal turned the second into swiss cheese. 
Third one made it through—Grinch caught an AK round to the leg. 
Through and through. He's screaming but he's alive. Helo touched 
down, miniguns blazing. We're out.

--- MISSION STATUS: SUCCESS ---
HVT: Secured
KIA: 0
WIA: 2 (Prophet - shoulder, Grinch - leg, both recoverable)
Enemy EKIA: 23 confirmed
